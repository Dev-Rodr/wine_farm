Quinta Casa Amarela

Some History...

The Quinta da Casa Amarela estate is situated on the left bank of the River Douro,in Cambres valley, in the spot classified by UNESCO as World Heritage, half the way between the cities of Régua and Lamego, in the heart of the most ancient enclosed region – the River Douro. It has belonged to the same family since 1885. The dwelling house was rebuilt and extended in the first quarter of the twentieth century and its yellow colour lends its name to the estate. It is a family enterprise that carries on centennial traditions in the region in what concerns the art of excellent production/making of Port Wine and wines marked as originating from Douro. Its activity lays down on four cornerstones:

  1. The development of a sustainable winegrowing
  2. The wine production
  3. Wine tourism
  4. Tourism in the Rural Area
