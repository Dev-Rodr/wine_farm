The Viticulture

It all starts in the vineyard….

The farm has a wine-growing heritage with an average age above 65 years old where the noblest grape varieties of the Douro are present: touriga nacional, touriga franca, tinta barroca, tinta roriz, tinto cão, tinta amarela, Tinta Amarela, Malvasia Fina, Viosinho, Côdega and others.

Installed on terraces supported by stone walls, where the plantation follows the contour lines.. In vineyards with these characteristics, the renovation is carried out with great care and attention in order not to cause sudden imbalances in the quality of the musts.
